## Profile {#profile}

Information systems researcher and educator specialising in **AI and digital transformation in organisations**. My research examines how organisations adopt, implement, and govern AI and digital technologies, and the consequences for work, equity, and decision-making. It is sociotechnical in orientation and combines quantitative, qualitative, and computational methods. Alongside this central focus I work on data governance and public-sector data, cybersecurity and data protection, and applied data analytics.

Seven semesters of university teaching in databases, web technologies, and programming, with strong student evaluations, and nine years of industry experience managing information systems and technology projects, let me connect research with practice and bring emerging technologies into the classroom.

**Research interests:** AI in organisations and algorithmic management · generative AI adoption and workplace inequality · digital transformation and data governance · cybersecurity and data protection · applied data analytics (machine learning, NLP, visualisation)
