## Research

My research examines how organisations adopt, implement, and govern AI and digital technologies, and the consequences for work, equity, and decision-making. It is sociotechnical in orientation and draws on quantitative, qualitative, and computational methods. The full list of publications is on my [CV](/cv.html) and [Google Scholar]({{ site.author.scholar }}).

### Research areas
- **AI in organisations and algorithmic management** — autonomy, power, leadership, and the employment relationship
- **Generative AI adoption and workplace inequality** — who uses generative AI at work, and the equity consequences
- **Digital transformation and data governance** — public-sector digitalisation and open government data
- **Cybersecurity and data protection** — predictive modelling of breach risk; security of health information systems
- **Applied data analytics** — machine learning, natural language processing, and data visualisation
